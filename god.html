God

In the beginning, God created the heavens and the earth. — Genesis 1:1

THE Bible opens with a pictorial and symbolic description of imagination in motion.

And God said, “Let there be light,” and there was light. — Genesis 1:3

This is the voice and the word of God.

Elohim is a plural name revealing the manifold aspects within a single creative source—your own consciousness. The bible shows that this “many within one” reflects the diverse inner powers and assumptions working together in your imagination to shape your reality. You are the unified source expressing through multiple inner faculties, creating by assumption.

These manifold aspects of awareness are personified in scripture as men and woman, kings, judges and rulers.
